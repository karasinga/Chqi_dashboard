Our Story

Our Chronological Journey

2020

CRISSP+ Launched

A PEPFAR/CDC funded project to build public health capacity in Kirinyaga and Kiambu for universal HIV prevention and treatment.

2021

Fahari ya Jamii Project

A PEPFAR/USAID-funded project to increase access to quality, county-led health services in Nairobi and Kajiado.

July 2022

Centre Commissioned

The Vice Chancellor, Prof. Stephen Kiama, officially commissioned our state-of-the-art Centre, a milestone for UoN.

2025

HIV Integration in Primary Care (HIP)

A Gates Foundation Initiative to integrate HIV services into primary healthcare in Nairobi, Kiambu, and Kitui.
